About

From sales and operations to data analytics.

My career did not begin in tech. It began with people, numbers, goals, and pressure: understanding customers, closing sales, managing expectations, and learning how business decisions show up in real life.

Sales taught me communication and ownership. Inventory and operations taught me accuracy, process, and the cost of messy information. Those experiences are the reason data analytics feels like a natural next step for me. I already know how much better a team can perform when it can trust the information in front of it.

I am now building the technical side of that foundation through Python, SQL, Tableau, dashboarding, AWS cloud services, software development, and machine learning fundamentals. My goal is to become a data analyst who can translate between business questions and technical answers.

I am especially interested in roles where data can improve operations, customer experience, sales performance, reporting, and decision-making. I am also open to cloud, software, and business-facing IT roles where my background can help a team move faster with more context.
